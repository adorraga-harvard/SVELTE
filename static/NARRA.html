MEMORANDUM FOR THE PRESIDENT

TO :      HIS EXCELLENCY, THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
FROM :      AMADOR M. RAGA
       Advocate for National Digital Transformation
DATE :      July 12, 2025
SUBJECT :      A BLUEPRINT FOR NATIONAL TRANSFORMATION THROUGH PROJECT "AVENIDA" AND THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE NATIONAL AUTHENTICATED REGISTRY AND REFERENCE AUTHORITY (NARRA)

I.    EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Your Excellency, this memorandum presents a legacy-defining opportunity to solve deep-rooted bureaucratic failures and construct the digital backbone of a modern, efficient, and truly sovereign Philippines. Our nation is burdened by digital friction, systemic vulnerabilities, and outdated processes that frustrate citizens and stifle economic growth. The most visible symptoms are the easily forged Senior Citizen and PWD cards and the frustrating delays in civil registration.

This blueprint offers a definitive, two-pronged solution:

1.   Launch Project AVENIDA (Archive and Verification Engine for National Identity and Data Administration): A national, time-bound project to build the secure digital infrastructure that redefines the PhilSys ID as a secure "key" and empowers Local Government Units as live, real-time gateways to the national database.

2.   Establish NARRA (National Authenticated Registry and Reference Authority): A fundamental institutional reform, replacing the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) with a new Authority built for the digital age—strong, secure, and permanent, like its namesake, our national tree.

By embracing this blueprint, your administration will not only solve long-standing problems but will be remembered for building the foundational infrastructure for generations of Filipino progress. This is the cornerstone of a Bagong Pilipinas.

II.    THE NATIONAL IMPERATIVE: FROM LEGACY FLAWS TO A FUTURE-PROOF FOUNDATION

The current system is a patchwork of insecure, siloed databases that creates two critical failures:

1.   It Undermines Trust and Dignity. The separate Senior Citizen and PWD ID systems are rife with fraud, leaking vital national funds and failing to afford our most vulnerable citizens the dignity of a secure and universally accepted identity. They are symbols of a broken process.

2.   It Creates Systemic Inefficiency. The antiquated "collect-and-submit" model for vital records (births, marriages, business permits) is a primary source of red tape. This friction costs our economy billions in lost productivity and our citizens countless hours of frustration.

The PhilSys was a visionary first step. Now, we must build the secure superhighway upon which it can travel to its full potential, solving these problems at their very root.

III.    THE BLUEPRINT: A TWO-PRONGED STRATEGY FOR NATIONAL TRANSFORMATION

This strategy is comprised of a dynamic project (the engine) and a permanent institution (the foundation).

PART A: Launch Project AVENIDA (The Engine of Transformation)

This will be a Presidential priority project with a clear mandate to build the nation's new digital infrastructure.

Mission 1: Redefine the National ID as a "Secure Key." The physical PhilSys card will be simplified, removing sensitive data. Its sole purpose is to be a secure key. Authentication will be digital: a scan of the card will ping the central NARRA database, which will instantly send back a secure, time-stamped token verifying a citizen's identity and current status (e.g., "VERIFIED SENIOR CITIZEN"). This single stroke renders existing fraudulent cards obsolete and eliminates the need for multiple IDs.

Mission 2: Empower LGUs as "Live Gateways." Project AVENIDA will equip every LGU with the standardized technology to connect directly to the national database. A birth registered in an LGU is entered once and is instantly part of the secure national registry. The LGU is transformed from a slow submission point into a vital, real-time node in the national network.

PART B: Establish NARRA (The Foundation of Trust)

To ensure this transformation is permanent, the PSA must be reformed into an institution built for this purpose.

Action: We propose the legislative creation of the National Authenticated Registry and Reference Authority (NARRA) to replace the PSA.

A Name with Profound Meaning: This name declares that our national identity system will be as strong, resilient, and enduring as our national tree, with each letter defining its mission:

  • National: It has supreme domestic authority.
  • Authenticated: Its core promise is to deliver genuine, fraud-proof identity verification.
  • Registry: It manages the single, unified, and authoritative list of all citizens.
  • Reference: It is the definitive source against which all other entities must check for identity verification.
  • Authority: It has the legal power to regulate and enforce national standards.

IV.    TRANSFORMATIVE NATIONAL IMPACT

The successful implementation of this blueprint will yield immediate and revolutionary results:

  • For Citizens: A single, secure ID for all transactions. Dignified and fraud-free access to benefits for Seniors and PWDs. The end of long waits for essential documents.
  • For Governance: The eradication of fraud in social benefit programs. Data-driven policymaking and unprecedented efficiency. A massive blow against corruption and red tape.
  • For the Economy: A hyper-efficient environment for business registration and commerce, built on a foundation of trusted digital identity that will attract investment.
  • For National Security: A robust and secure identity framework that enhances law enforcement, border control, and disaster response.

V.    AN URGENT AND RESPECTFUL CALL TO ACTION

Your Excellency, the vision is clear, the technology is available, and the moment to act is now. We respectfully urge you to:

1.   Issue an Executive Order creating the Project AVENIDA Task Force, under the direct supervision of the Office of the President, with a 90-day mandate to draft the full implementation plan, including the necessary legislative proposals and budget for the establishment of NARRA.

2.   Publicly champion this vision as a cornerstone of your administration's legacy. Your leadership is the key to galvanizing the political will needed to dismantle bureaucratic resistance.

This is more than an IT project; it is a nation-building endeavor. With your decisive leadership, we can build the digital republic our people have long deserved—one that is secure, swift, and the pride of Southeast Asia.

Respectfully submitted for Your Excellency's consideration and bold action.
